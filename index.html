About Me

A true passionate of everything related to cloud, infrastructure, DevOps and automation, I like to use my software engineering skills to make everyone's life easier.

I collaborate with my customers to uncover their needs and identify the most efficient solutions, with an emphasis on hiding complexity and reducing time-to-market while making maintenance easy at the same time.

Proactive and curious, I love to learn new skills on a daily basis and I embrace change whenever it shows up.

In spite of being very open to new ideas, I am very strict and uncompromising when it comes to writing clean code and documenting my work.

Finally, as a big fan of open source in general, I would rather reuse existing components than reinvent the wheel.
